# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-16

## 1. Overview
**ImageMax** ("the App") is designed with privacy as its core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information or photos.

## 2. Data Collection
*   **No Personal Data:** We do not collect your name, email, or any other personal details.
*   **No Usage Tracking:** We do not track how you use the App.
*   **No Image Uploads:** The App operates 1000ffline. Your photos are processed locally on your device using Apple Neural Engine and never leave your computer.

## 3. Local Processing
All AI upscaling and image processing tasks are performed on your device's hardware. No third-party servers are involved.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our [Support Page](https://github.com/jlvihv/ImageMax-Support).
